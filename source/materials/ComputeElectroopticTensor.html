ComputeElectroopticTensor

Compute an electrooptic tensor.

Overview

Stores the electrooptic tensor components . The tensor may be rotated via,

with the internal RotationTensor operation in MOOSE utils. The rotation operator accepts Euler angles in the standard Bunge sequence ().
