BulkEnergyCoupledT

buildconstruction:Undocumented Class

The BulkEnergyCoupledT has not been documented. The content listed below should be used as a starting point for documenting the class, which includes the typical automatic documentation associated with a MooseObject; however, what is contained is ultimately determined by what is necessary to make the documentation clear for users.

Calculates an integral over the sixth order free energy density of local polarization coupled to temperature.
