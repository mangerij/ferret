DepolarizationEnergy

Calculates an integral over a fictious depolarization field energy density

Overview

Computes a volume integral over the free energy density defined by,

with a screening length. The value is a volume averaged quantity computed from ElementAverageValue. This piece of code is restrictive in the sense that it is hardcoded for only the direction. It can be generalized further if needed.
