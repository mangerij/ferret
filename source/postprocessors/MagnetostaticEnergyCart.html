MagnetostaticEnergyCart

Overview

Computes the free energy of the simulation box (volume ) corresponding to magnetostatic energy,

where is the permeability of vacuum, is the saturation magnetization density, the normalized magnetization and the magnetostatic potential. An optional input parameter flag energyscale is provided allowing to quickly convert the postprocessed units.
